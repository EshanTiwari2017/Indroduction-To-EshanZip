Introduction to EshanZip

EshanZip is a modern file archiver created to make compression and extraction simple, clean, and efficient. It supports both the universal .zip format and its own branded archive format, .oho.

Just like how .sb3 (Scratch projects) and .docx (Microsoft Word documents) are essentially .zip files under the hood, .oho archives are also .zip files — but given a unique identity for the Eshan ecosystem.

With EshanZip, you can:

  • Create new .oho archives
  • Open existing .oho or .zip archives
  • Add, extract, or delete files inside the archive

EshanZip is designed to be lightweight, user-friendly, and uniquely yours — giving .oho files the same recognition as other well-known compressed formats.
